Our Story

Why We Built IronScale

We kept seeing the same thing — incredible powerlifting gyms and bodybuilding-focused training facilities with loyal communities, great equipment, and experienced coaches — completely invisible online. No ads. Dead social media. A website that looked like it was built in 2009.

Meanwhile, the people who were actively searching for exactly those gyms couldn't find them. Members were going to Planet Fitness because it was the first result on Google — not because they wanted to be there.

We built IronScale to close that gap. A marketing agency that understands the strength world, speaks the language, and knows how to reach the people who actually want what these gyms offer.
